My Past Projects

SkConnect

A digital platform designed to promote transparency among SK (Sangguniang Kabataan) officials by showcasing events, budgets, and members. It helps future administrations learn from past mistakes and improve activities.

Alertify

Alertify uses AI-powered facial, audio, and behavioral analysis to monitor participants’ alertness, engagement, and focus during virtual meetings. It provides real-time feedback and post-meeting analytics to boost productivity.

MoodBot

A web-based AI chatbot that detects a user's emotional state through text or voice and responds with personalized quotes, affirmations, or advice tailored to their mood.

EggLink

EggLink is a digital marketplace platform that helps farmers, distributors, and buyers across the country connect, sell, and buy agricultural products, focusing primarily on egg-based goods (chicken eggs, duck eggs, quail eggs, etc.) and gradually expanding to related poultry or agricultural products.

Cisco Network Design

A network design and implementation proposal for Barangay Union, Ubay, Bohol. The goal is to digitalize and modernize data management while addressing connectivity challenges.
